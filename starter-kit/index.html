Inside the kit

Six steps, one per day.

Each step is a single page in the PDF: a clear goal, a 3-4 item checklist, a real example, and a "watch out for" warning from people who've made the mistake.

01 MONDAY · 30 MIN

Pick ONE data domain.

The biggest reason governance fails is trying to govern everything. Pick the one that hurts most when wrong.

02 TUESDAY · 1 HR

Name a real human.

"The data team owns it" means nobody owns it. Every domain needs an accountable owner and a steward — by name.

03 WEDNESDAY · 1 HR

Define your top 5.

Five fields or metrics that get debated or break things. Plain-language definitions. Source of truth. Done.

04 THURSDAY · 30 MIN

Set your access rules.

Decide who sees, edits, and changes what. Default to minimum access. Audit quarterly. This is the boring part that prevents fines.

05 FRIDAY · 1 HR

Pick 3 quality checks.

Counts, nulls, freshness. Alerts to a human, not a forgotten Slack channel. Three you act on beat fifty you ignore.

06 FRIDAY PM · 15 MIN

Schedule the review.

Monthly. 30 minutes. Standing agenda. Hard cap. Without this, governance dies in 3 months. With it, it compounds.
